Navigation app tailor made for camper van travellers

We know that when it comes to choosing the route for your journey it's always difficult. Therefore, our app provides you with a solution. Just enter your destination and 3 alternatives are going to be provided, alternatives already explored before by other users, to ensure a smooth and beautiful trip according to your needs: most fuel-efficient, highways and the most beautiful option.

Travelling with your friends and or family is for sure lovely, but why not connect with other users of our app to gain useful insights and new friends? Just text the user you want via our app and read their reply or maybe meet them, to hear about other remote places, road quality and famous landmarks.
